About
[B42] Daily Challenges & Leaderboard [SP/MP]
Daily Challenges & Leaderboard gives your Project Zomboid playthrough a brand-new progression system which incentivizes Players to keep logging in Daily. Every day at Midnight (UTC), 7 new Challenges become available for Players to complete. Complete Challenges to earn tokens and then exchange those tokens for Items at the 'Token Vendor'.
Challenge Types
Challenges are divided into 7 Categories:
Kills/Weapon: Slay Zombies with anything from a Broom to an M16 Assault Rifle. Challenges range from generic Zombie killing headcounts to weapon-specific kill counts covering almost every melee weapon, firearm, and improvised weapon in the game. (318 Challenges)
Quest/Deliver: Find the required item(s), travel to the location and deposit them into the designated Safe. (93 Items across 1,193 Locations = 110,949 unique Challenge combinations)
Find the Gnome: David's cousin, Barney the Gnome has landed in Knox Country. Head to the location and find Barney to complete the challenge. (1,193 Locations)
Eat/Drink: Cook and consume specific meals, find and consume non-perishable food and drinks or forage/farm and consume some fresh produce. (168 Challenges)
Fish/Hunt/Forage: Reel in a specific fish species, hunt a wild animal, or forage for materials and supplies in the wilderness. (52 Challenges)
Craft/Build: Craft tools, weapons, and clothes from raw materials, or build walls, fences, doors, crates, and other structures. (223 Challenges)
Miscellaneous: Travel a set distance by foot or in a Vehicle. Read books and magazines. Change your hairstyle or apply makeup. Install or remove vehicle parts and more. (122 Challenges)
Streaks & Rewards
Complete 1 Challenge per day to maintain a Streak. Maintaining a Daily Streak pushes you through 4 reward tiers over 28 days. Early tiers pay 1 Token per Challenge; the final tier pays 4 Tokens per Challenge. Finish all 7 Challenges in one day for bonus Tokens. Complete a 28 day Streak for a 25-token milestone before the cycle resets.
Token Vendors
Exchange Tokens for Items. Available Items and quantities are randomized daily. Two random Vendor locations spawn each day — one on the east side of the map, one on the west. Each one carries different items. Complete all 7 Challenges per day to unlock their Locations.
Leaderboard/Hall of Fame
Earn your place on the Leaderboard by competing with other Players on the Server for the Most Challenges Completed, Longest Streak, Fastest Challenge Completion time and more. In Singleplayer, the Hall of Fame displays your Character's best efforts in Most Challenges Complete and Fastest Completion Times.
Admin Tools
In Multiplayer, Admin enabled accounts can access the Admin Panel which allows them to control Data Backup + Restore, Shop stock editing, Leaderboard Reset, and more Tools to manage server-wide and per-user statistics. In Singleplayer, these Admin Tools can be accessed by launching the game in Debug Mode.
Compatibility
- Build 42.15+
- Compatible with Multiplayer (Dedicated & Hosted) and Singleplayer
- Server authoritative validation to prevent client-side exploits.
- Compatible with most other Mods. All Items from other Mods are available to be added to the Token Vendor Shop via the Admin's 'Vendor Editor' panel. May have some visual conflicts with Mods that add a sidebar icon or Mods that add a button to the Vanilla PZ Admin Menu. Modded Weapons at this time do not count towards kill challenges (but may be supported in Future Updates).
- Supports all Font Size Settings.
- Supported Languages: EN, FR, DE, ES, PTBR, RU, AR, CN
